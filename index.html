SmartGlove AI: Technical Architecture

Restricted Access Document v2.1

1. Core Technology

SmartGlove operates on a decentralized edge computing model. Unlike cloud-based translators that require internet latency, our proprietary SG-1 Chip processes tensor calculations directly on the wrist.

2. Sensor Fusion

We utilize a combination of 5-axis flex sensors and 9-axis IMUs (Inertial Measurement Units) to capture micro-movements of the phalanges.

3. Clinical Trials

Tested at IMS-BHU Hospital, the device demonstrated a 40% increase in social interaction frequency among mute patients over a 3-month period.
